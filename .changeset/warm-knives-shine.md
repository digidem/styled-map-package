Initial release. Pre-built Noto Sans SDF glyph PBFs (80+ scripts) for use as fallback glyphs in MapLibre-compatible map servers. Built from GoNotoKurrent covering Latin, Cyrillic, Greek, Arabic, Hebrew, Devanagari, Thai, and many more. CJK/Hangul ranges excluded (rendered client-side by MapLibre).
